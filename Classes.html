Classes

Introduction to Boxing

This class will be an introduction to boxing aimed at beginners. The course will begin to go over the basics of boxing and how to cover, move, and strike while in the ring. By the end of this course you will have a grasp of how to carry yourself in a boxing match.

Introduction to Weightlifting

This is less of a course and more of a one time walk around with multiple trainers that will show you how to properly use the equipment that we offer to avoid injure to your self and others around you.

Introduction to Wrestling

This course will offer the basics of wrestling and how to graple with your opponent. This is only an introduction so many of the moves taught are eassy to understand and practice. With most introduciton classes you will only be learing how to get into the sport.
